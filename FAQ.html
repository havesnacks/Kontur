OUR CUSTOMIZATION PROCESS

WE WORK CLOSELY WITH COMMERCIAL CLIENTS TO UNDERSTAND THEIR NEEDS. UPON INQUIRY, WHETHER IT'S A COMPANY LOGO, ACCENT ARTWORK, OR EVEN A MIX OF BOTH. MEASUREMENTS WILL ALSO BE TAKEN OR REQUESTED AT THE CONSTRUCTION SPACE AS WELL TO DETERMINE PROJECT COST.

ONCE A DEPOSIT IS PLACED, WILL DESIGN CUSTOM ARTWORK TO COMPLIMENT THE SPACE IN CONSTRUCTION, THIS INCLUDES CONCEPTUALIZATION, MOCK-UPS, AND ADJUSTMENTS BASED ON CLIENT FEEDBACK.

ONCE THE DESIGN AND PLAN ARE APPROVED BY THE CLIENT, IT WILL TAKE ROUGHLY 7-10 BUSINESS DAYS FOR THE PRODUCT TO BE COMPLETED AND READY FOR SHIPPING OR INSTALLATION. PLEASE SEE INSTALLATION INFORMATION BELOW.

ENGINEERED WITH SIMPLICITY IN MIND, AND ENSURING MINIMAL DISRUPTION TO THE COMMERCIAL SPACE, ALL PANELS MUST BE INSTALLED BY INDUSTRY PROFESSIONALS. WE ALSO PROVIDE AN OPTIONAL INSTALLATION SERVICE WITHIN THE GTA UPON REQUEST BY OUR LICENSED CONTRACTING PARTNER. WE WILL ALSO PROVIDE A PDF OF INSTALLATION DETAILS. CONTACT US FOR MORE INFORMATION.
